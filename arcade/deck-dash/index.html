How to Play

Objective

Your slide deck has gone haywire mid-presentation. Run across the slide and dodge typos, pop-ups, and stray notifications before you run out of lives.

Controls

  • Space or tap the slide to jump over low obstacles.
  • Down Arrow to duck under obstacles that fly overhead.

Roles & Difficulty

Intern / Manager / Director change your starting lives and how fast the deck scrolls. Harder roles also score more points.

Levels

There are 10 levels, each a stretch of distance to survive. From Level 6 onward, a faster "Notification Chaser" obstacle appears — it costs 2 lives instead of 1 if it catches you, and both scroll speed and chaser speed increase every level.

Save, Resume & Reset

  • Progress auto-saves after every level you clear.
  • Reopening this page resumes your run automatically.
  • Reset wipes your current run and lets you pick a new role. Top 10 records are kept.

Records & Scoring

Score = distance covered + Level reached × 200 + a difficulty bonus (Manager +50, Director +150). Reaching #1 automatically downloads a screenshot of your run.
