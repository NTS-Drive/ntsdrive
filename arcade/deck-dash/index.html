How to Play

Objective

It's 6PM and everyone's racing for the exit. Dash home through three acts of office chaos — Word, then Excel, then PPT — before you run out of lives.

Controls

  • Space or tap the screen to jump — press again in mid-air for a double jump.
  • Down Arrow to duck under obstacles that fly overhead.

Three obstacle heights

Obstacles can appear at three different heights, so read each one before you react:

  • Ground — jump over it.
  • Head height — duck under it; jumping through this zone is risky.
  • Sky high — only a double jump reaches this far up. Stay grounded (or duck) and let it pass.

Food = energy + score

Snacks (🍙🍎☕🍪🍱) float by occasionally. Grab one for +30 score, and it also restores a life if you're not already at full health for your role.

Three Acts

Levels 1–3 (Word): dodge word cards — short ones first, then longer ones like "DEADLINE" (shown as a folded paper card).
Levels 4–7 (Excel): dodge numbers (shown as a spreadsheet cell), then bar and pie chart shapes.
Levels 8–10 (PPT): dodge geometric shapes — circle, triangle, arrow, star.

The toolbar color changes to match the current act, so you always know where you are at a glance.

Roles & Difficulty

Intern / Manager / Director change your starting lives and how fast the deck scrolls. Harder roles also score more points.

Level 6+

From Level 6 onward, a faster "Notification Chaser" also appears on the ground — it costs 2 lives instead of 1 if it catches you.

Endless Mode (Level 10+)

Once you clear Level 10, leveling stops — from then on it's pure distance. Obstacles spawn 1.5× more often and snacks stop appearing entirely, so it's just you, your reflexes, and the road home. Reach 999,999m and you become a Master — a special achievement screen celebrates it.

Save, Resume & Reset

  • Progress auto-saves after every level you clear.
  • Reopening this page resumes your run automatically.
  • Reset wipes your current run and lets you pick a new role. Top 10 records are kept.

Records & Scoring

Score = distance covered + Level reached × 200 + a difficulty bonus (Manager +50, Director +150) + 30 per snack collected. The Top 10 is ranked purely by score (distance is shown alongside for context but isn't the ranking metric). Reaching #1 automatically downloads a screenshot of your run.
