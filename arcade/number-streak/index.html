How to Play

Objective

The left box shows the current number. Guess whether the next random number will be Lower, the same (Draw), or Higher.

Rounds & Levels

Each level is exactly 10 rounds (see the table below the buttons — it fills in with O for a hit or X for a miss as you go). Clear a level's score target within those 10 rounds to advance; fall short and the run ends.

Draws get much rarer at wide ranges, so the draw bonus scales up to compensate — weigh that into your guess.

Streak bonus

Two correct guesses in a row or more multiplies that round's points: multiplier = 1 + 0.1 × (streak − 1), capped at 2.0×. A single hit (streak of 1) earns the normal 10 points; a miss resets your streak to zero. Scores are shown to 1 decimal place.

Draw bonus

Correctly calling a Draw multiplies that round's points on top of any streak bonus, using the table above.

Save, Resume & Reset

  • Progress auto-saves after every round.
  • Reopening this page resumes your run automatically.
  • Reset wipes your current run and starts over from Level 1. Top 10 records are kept.
