How to Play

Objective

You're an office worker who'd rather be anywhere but at your desk. Eat money to grow stronger and avoid your work — hitting a wall, your own tail, or an obstacle costs a life.

Controls

Arrow keys / WASD, or the on-screen Up / Down / Left / Right buttons. You can't reverse directly into yourself.

Getting started

After picking a role, a 5-second countdown gives you a moment to look at the board before the snake starts moving. Losing a life triggers a shorter 3-second countdown before you're back in control — you keep your current length when you respawn, you don't shrink back down.

Roles

Intern / Manager / Director only change your starting lives (5 / 3 / 2). Losing a life respawns you at a safe spot in the same level — it doesn't erase your progress toward that level's target.

Obstacles vs. coins

To keep them easy to tell apart at a glance, obstacles are plain colored blocks (the same color as the title bar), while coins and bonus items are always emoji.

Speed

Every 50 points adds another +10% to your speed for the rest of that level (shown live next to your score), on top of each level already being a little faster than the last.

Safe respawns

When you respawn, the game deliberately avoids placing you facing straight into an obstacle — it keeps searching for a spot where the way ahead is clear.

3 Levels

  • Level 1 — No obstacles. Eat 5 coins to advance. 1 bonus coin (💎) is on the board — eating it is worth the usual points and also restores a life (if not already full).
  • Level 2 — 4 office-equipment obstacles appear on the board. Eat 10 coins to advance. 2 bonus coins: one restores a life, the other is worth 1.5× points.
  • Level 3 — Level 2's obstacles plus 4 larger "company building" obstacles. No bonus coins here. Instead, every coin you eat without touching a wall or your own tail adds to a combo — the Nth coin in an unbroken streak is worth an extra N% (so a 10-coin streak's next coin is worth +10%). Hitting a wall or your own tail resets the combo — hitting an obstacle does not. Eating 100 coins in Level 3 makes you a Payroll Master — a special ending screen celebrates it.

Save, Resume & Reset

  • Progress auto-saves after every coin eaten.
  • Reopening this page resumes your run automatically.
  • Reset wipes your current run and lets you pick a new role. Top 10 records are kept.

Records

The Top 10 is ranked by score. Reaching the all-time #1 automatically downloads a screenshot of your run.
