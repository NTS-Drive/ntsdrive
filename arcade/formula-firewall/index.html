How to Play

Objective

Corrupted data errors are marching across your spreadsheet toward the edge of the sheet. Deploy formula towers to stop them before your Integrity hits zero.

Controls

  • Select a tower in the ribbon, then click an empty cell (not on the shaded path) to place it.
  • Click Start Wave to release that level's threats.
  • Defeated threats refund some Budget so you can afford more towers.
  • Click 🔨 Demolish, then click any of your towers to remove it for a 50% refund of its cost.

Towers

  • =VLOOKUP() — cheap, balanced, good early pick.
  • =IFERROR() — fires rapidly but hits softer, good against fast threats.
  • =SUMIF() — expensive, long range, heavy single-target damage.
  • =INDEX() — unlocks at Level 4. Precision sniper: long range, strong single-target hits.
  • =QUERY() — unlocks at Level 7. Area attack: hits every threat in range at once.

Tower Energy

Each tower has its own Energy bar (shown just below it), starting at a value equal to its cost. When a threat attacks a tower, it's that tower's Energy — not your overall Integrity — that drains. If a tower's Energy hits zero, it explodes and is removed from the board for good. Energy damage is cumulative across the whole run (it does not refill between levels), so keep an eye on towers that have taken repeated hits.

Roles & Difficulty

Intern / Manager / Director change your starting Budget, how fast & strong threats are, and how much starting Energy your towers get (Intern towers start tougher, to offset threats attacking from Level 3). Higher difficulty also scores more points.

Levels & Threats

There are 10 levels. From Level 3 onward, threats that get close to one of your towers will actively attack its Energy. From Level 6 onward, two special threats can appear: a slow, heavily-armored Corrupted Cell (#REF!), and a fast, harmless Bonus Byte that pays out extra Budget when destroyed (and costs nothing if it slips through). Threat speed and attack damage both increase every level.

Save, Resume & Reset

  • Progress auto-saves after every level you clear.
  • Reopening this page resumes your run automatically — no need to pick a role again.
  • Reset wipes your current run and lets you pick a new role. Your Top 10 records are kept.

Records & Scoring

Score = Level reached × 1000 + Integrity remaining × 5 + a difficulty bonus (Manager +50, Director +150). The Top 10 scores are stored on this device. If your score qualifies, you'll be asked for a name. Reaching #1 automatically downloads a screenshot of your victory to your device.
