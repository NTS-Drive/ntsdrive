How to Play

Objective

Each level hides a calculation (like 5*1-4) that equals the target number shown at the top. Guess the exact calculation, character by character.

Feedback

Correct character in the correct spot   Correct character, wrong spot   Not in the calculation at all.

Rules

Calculations alternate digit / operator / digit / operator... standard order of operations applies (× and ÷ before + and −). No parentheses, no multi-digit numbers.

Levels

Levels 1–3 use 5-character calculations with 8 guesses. Levels 4–7 use 7 characters with 6 guesses. Levels 8–10 use 9 characters with 5 guesses. Solve a level to advance; running out of guesses ends your run at that level.

Progress

Your best level reached is saved on this device — no need to restart from Level 1 to beat your record next time you play.
